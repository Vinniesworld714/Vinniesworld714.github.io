 Vincent Agro is an American artist and photographer. An emphasis on expressive stillness and explorative narrative allow Vincent's works to perpetually evolve inside of the human imagination. Being a passionate, lifelong musician and artist studying composition in both fields extensively, these interests tie in with a benevolent understanding and study of the arts, philosophy, psychology, the interplay of life, and the reflective aspects of art.
